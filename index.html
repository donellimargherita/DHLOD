What is DHLOD?

A project proposal for the enhancment of a cultural heritage collection via the upgrade of the platform Digital Humanties.

The goal is the conversion of a sample set of heterogeneous cultural artefacts extracted from the object-oriented database of the Fondazione Carisbo, into Linked Open Data (LOD) format.
